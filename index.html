ABOUT ME

I'm a freelance developer located in Kerala, India. I enjoy building everything from small business sites to rich interactive web apps. When I'm not coding, you'll find me with my friends and family.

Over the past years, I've developed wide range of websites using PHP, HTML, AJAX, jQuery, CSS and MySQL including sites for start-up companies, personal blogs, e-commerce and social networking. I strive to create integrated strategies to develop and expand existing customer sales, brand or product evolution, and media endorsement.

I've been freelancing since 2010 and I understand the challenges of working remotely and I overcome this with my Communication, Time Keeping and Project Management skills. I can be available for 40+ hours per week, can be reached online from Monday through Friday in flexible hours and my hourly rates are negotiable.

I'm available for remote work - if you would like to build something together, get in touch?
